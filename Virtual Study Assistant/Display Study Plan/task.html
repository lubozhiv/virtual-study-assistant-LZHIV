Theory

Congratulations on completing the first stage! Now that you have a study plan, it’s time to display it in a readable format. Organizing your plan not only helps you keep track of your schedule but also ensures you can visualize the effort required for each subject. This is a crucial step in staying motivated and prepared.

In this stage, you’ll also calculate the total study time, including breaks. This will give you a better idea of how much time you need to set aside for your studies.

Description

Your task is to print the saved study plan with subjects and their allocated times in a predefined format. Additionally, calculate the total study time and include 15-minute breaks after every 45 minutes of study. This will make your schedule more realistic and easier to follow.

Objectives

  • Print the study plan in a formatted way, showing each subject and its allocated time.

  • Calculate the total study time by summing the times for all subjects.

  • Add 15-minute breaks for every 45 minutes of study time.

Examples

Input (from previous stage):

subjects = {"Math": 90, "Science": 120}

Output:

Your study plan:
Math: 90 minutes
Science: 120 minutes
Total study time: 210 minutes
Total time including breaks: 270 minutes
